Vakhareli Khichadi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal (lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as needed

Steps:

  1. Preparation:
    • Rinse the rice and lentils separately under running water until the water runs clear. Soak them separately in water for about 20-30 minutes. Drain before using.
  2. Cooking Vakhareli Khichadi:
    • Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker or a heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
    • Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
    • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
    • Put grated ginger, minced garlic, and chopped green chilies into the pot. Sauté for a minute.
    • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
